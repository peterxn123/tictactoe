How to Play

Tic-Tac-Toe is a simple game where two players take turns marking "X" and "O" in a 3x3 grid. The first player to align three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins. If all 9 squares are filled without a winner, the game ends in a draw.
